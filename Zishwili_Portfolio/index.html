About Me

2026
BSc Graduate
EMCU

I am a passionate and disciplined Medical Laboratory Scientist, graduating with Second Class Upper Division (CGPA: 4.36/5.50) from Eswatini Medical Christian University (EMCU). Every accurate test result, I believe, is a critical step toward saving a life.

My 6-month clinical placement at Cerba Lancet Eswatini and Eswatini Hospital Laboratory Services (EHLS) transformed academic knowledge into practical expertise within ISO 15189-accredited, high-throughput diagnostic settings.

I also designed and executed an independent research project — a retrospective cross-sectional study on the Prevalence of Hepatitis B Virus in Pregnant Women at Mbabane Public Health Unit, which yielded evidence-based policy recommendations for antenatal care in Eswatini.
